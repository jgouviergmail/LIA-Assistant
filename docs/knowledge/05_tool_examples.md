# Email, Calendar & Contacts Examples

## How do I search my contacts?
**Google Contacts** - Talk naturally to LIA:

**🔍 Simple search:**
• "*Find John's contact*"
• "*Show me Mary Smith's phone number*"
• "*What's Peter's email?*"

**🔍 Advanced search:**
• "*Find all contacts at Acme Corp*"
• "*Search for contacts with a number starting with 555*"
• "*List my contacts in New York*"

**📋 Results display:**
LIA displays a card for each contact with name, photo, phones, emails and organization. Photos are clickable and display larger (2x zoom) in a modal window.

**💡 Tip:**
You can then say "*the first one*" or "*the one from company X*" to reference a contact.

• "*Open the Q3 report document*"

• "*Read the first file*"

**📊 Supported file types:**

## What is the draft and approval system?
To protect your data, any modification action goes through a **draft system**:

**🔒 How it works:**
1. You request an action ("*Send an email to John*")
2. LIA prepares a **draft**
3. You see a **detailed preview**
4. Three options:
   • ✅ **Approve**: the action is executed
   • ✏️ **Edit**: correct the draft
   • ❌ **Cancel**: nothing is done

**📋 Actions concerned:**
• Sending, replying, forwarding emails
• Creating, modifying, deleting events
• Creating, modifying contacts
• Creating, completing tasks
• Deleting emails

**🛡️ Benefits:**
• Verification before every important action
• Ability to correct errors
• Full control over your data
• No irreversible action without your agreement

## What do the colors on email cards mean?
**Gmail** - Email color coding:

Email cards use a **colored border on the left side** to indicate their status:

**📧 Color code:**
• No color: **Read** email (no special marking)
• 🟠 Orange: **Read and important** email (marked important by Gmail)
• 🟢 Green: **Unread** email (new message to read)
• 🔴 Red: **Unread AND important** email (high priority)

**💡 Good to know:**
• The "*important*" status is automatically assigned by Gmail based on your habits
• Unread emails also have their **subject in bold** for better visibility
• The "*Unread*" badge also appears in the card's metadata

**📌 Quick summary:**
🟢 Green = To read | 🔴 Red = Urgent | 🟠 Orange = Important already read | Transparent = Already read

## How do I send an email?
**Gmail** - Sending emails with preview:

**📤 Simple send:**
• "*Send an email to john@example.com: Hello, I confirm our appointment tomorrow.*"

**📤 Structured send:**
• "*Send an email to mary@example.com with subject 'Project meeting' and content: Hello Mary, can you confirm your availability for Friday's meeting?*"

**📤 With CC:**
• "*Send an email to peter@example.com CC john@example.com about the monthly report*"

**✅ Sending process:**
1. LIA generates a **draft**
2. You see the **complete preview** (recipient, subject, content)
3. You can **approve**, **modify** or **cancel**
4. The email is sent only after your approval

**⚠️ Security tip:**
Always verify the recipient's email address in the preview!

## How do I delete an email?
**Gmail** - Secure deletion:

**🗑️ Delete an email:**
• "*Delete the spam email from yesterday*"
• "*Move the first email to trash*"
• "*Delete the promotional email from this morning*"

**⚠️ Secure process:**
1. LIA shows you the email to be deleted
2. You see the sender, subject and date
3. Confirmation required before deletion
4. The email is moved to **trash** (not permanently deleted)

**♻️ Recovery possible:**
Emails in trash are kept for 30 days by Gmail before permanent deletion.

**💡 Tip:**
To permanently delete, you must do it directly in Gmail.
