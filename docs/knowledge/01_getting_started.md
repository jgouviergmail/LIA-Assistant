# Getting Started

## What is LIA?
LIA (**L**earning **I**ntelligent **A**ssistant) is your personal intelligent assistant powered by artificial intelligence. LIA helps you daily with:

• **Managing your emails**: search, read, send, reply and forward
• **Organizing your calendar**: create, modify and view your events
• **Managing your contacts**: search, create and update
• **Managing your tasks**: create, track and complete your to-dos
• **Exploring your files**: search and read your Drive documents
• **Getting information**: weather, Wikipedia, web search, places

LIA understands natural language: talk to it like you would a human assistant!

## How do I start a conversation with LIA?
It's simple in 3 steps:

1. Click on **Chat** in the left menu
2. Type your message in the input box at the bottom of the screen
3. Press **Enter** to send

**💡 Tip**: Be natural! LIA understands phrases like "*Show me my emails from this week*" or "*What do I have scheduled tomorrow?*"

## Does LIA support multiple languages?
Yes! LIA supports **6 languages**:

🇫🇷 French (Français)
🇬🇧 English
🇪🇸 Spanish (Español)
🇩🇪 German (Deutsch)
🇮🇹 Italian (Italiano)
🇨🇳 Simplified Chinese (简体中文)

To change language:
• Click the **globe icon** 🌐 in the title bar
• Or go to **Settings > Appearance > Language**

LIA will respond in the interface language.

## How does the approval system work?
To protect your data, LIA uses an **intelligent approval system (Human-in-the-Loop)**. Before any action that modifies your data, LIA asks for confirmation:

**🔒 Actions requiring approval:**
• Send, reply to or forward an email
• Create, modify or delete an event
• Create, modify or delete a contact
• Create or complete a task

**📋 How it works:**
1. LIA prepares a **draft** of the action
2. You see a **detailed preview** of what will be done
3. You can:
   • ✅ **Approve**: the action is executed
   • ✏️ **Edit**: correct before validation
   • ❌ **Cancel**: nothing is done

**💡 Tip**: Take time to review the preview, especially for emails!

## Can LIA make mistakes?
Like any AI assistant, LIA may sometimes misinterpret a request. Here's how to get the best results:

**✅ Best practices:**
• Be specific: "*Send an email to john.smith@email.com*" rather than "*Send an email to John*"
• Give context: "*Find my emails from this week about the Alpha project*"
• Review previews before approving actions

**⚠️ If there's an error:**
• Rephrase your request with more details
• Use specific dates ("*January 15*") rather than relative ones ("*last week*")
• For important emails, always review the preview

**🛡️ Built-in security:**
Thanks to the approval system, no critical action is executed without your explicit validation.
