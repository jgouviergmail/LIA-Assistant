# Other Questions

## How much does using LIA cost?
LIA pricing depends on your organization:

**💰 Cost model:**
Costs are based on AI usage:
• **Input tokens**: your messages and analyzed data
• **Output tokens**: LIA's responses
• **Cache**: optimized data reuse

**📊 Influencing factors:**
• Length of your messages
• Request complexity
• Volume of data processed (emails, files...)
• Frequency of use

**📧 Pricing information:**
Contact your administrator for pricing applicable to your organization.

## How can I view my usage?
Track your usage from the **dashboard**:

**📊 Available statistics:**
• **Messages**: total count and current month
• **Tokens**: detailed consumption (input/output/cache)
• **Cost**: monthly cost estimate

**📅 Periods:**
• Total cumulative since account creation
• Current month (billing cycle)
• Current period with start/end dates

**🔄 Update:**
Statistics are updated in real-time after each conversation.

**💡 Access:**
Statistics are displayed directly on the home page (dashboard).

## Does LIA work on mobile?
Yes, LIA is **fully responsive**:

**📱 Compatibility:**
• Smartphones (iOS and Android)
• Tablets
• Computers (Windows, Mac, Linux)
• All modern browsers

**🔄 Synchronization:**
• Your conversation syncs in real-time
• Start on PC, continue on mobile
• Same preferences across all devices

**💡 Mobile tip:**
Add LIA to your home screen for quick access like a native app.

**⚠️ Internet connection:**
LIA requires an active internet connection to function.

## Are there usage limits?
Some technical limits exist to ensure performance:

**📊 Limits per request:**
• **Emails**: 50 results maximum per search
• **Contacts**: 50 results maximum per search
• **Drive files**: 50 results maximum
• **Events**: 50 results maximum

**📝 Message size:**
• Your messages can be long (multiple paragraphs)
• Very long content (files) is truncated

**⏱️ No time limit:**
• No limit on messages per day
• No restriction on hours of use

**💡 Tip:**
For broad searches, refine your criteria rather than asking for "all my emails".
